# Finding record

State the claim, observed behavior, expected behavior, and minimal
reproduction. Cite immutable evidence records. Assign severity from impact and
reachability, and confidence from evidence quality rather than rhetoric.

Describe business consequence, affected boundary, counterevidence considered,
and what new evidence would change the conclusion. Give an owner and one
implementation-ready next action. A failed control without a finding is an
incomplete engagement record.
